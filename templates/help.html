Help & Rules

Learn how to play Fantasy Champions League

Building Your Team

Starting Budget: 100M€

You must select 15 players total: 11 starting players and 4 bench players.

Formation Requirements

Position Starting XI Total (incl. bench)
Goalkeeper (GK) 1 2
Defender (DEF) 3-5 5
Midfielder (MID) 2-5 5
Forward (FWD) 1-3 3

Important:

  • You must have exactly 11 starting players
  • You must have exactly 4 bench players
  • Only starting players earn points

Scoring System

Points Earned

Action Details Points
Goal scored Any player +5
Assist Any player +3
Playing time Per 30 minutes played +1
Clean sheet GK/DEF only, 60+ min, 0 goals conceded +4

Points Lost

Action Details Points
Yellow card Any player -1
Red card Any player -3
Goal conceded GK/DEF only, per goal -1

Tips

  • • Balance your budget across all positions
  • • Check player status before selecting (Active players are more likely to play)
  • • You can swap players between your starting lineup and bench
  • • Monitor league rankings to track your performance
